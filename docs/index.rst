kRPC.MechJeb Documentation
==========================

Please bear in mind that the addon is in Alpha stage and may contain bugs.

This addon to `KRPC <https://krpc.github.io/krpc>`_ (a `Kerbal Space Program <https://kerbalspaceprogram.com/>`_ mod) provides RPCs to interact with `MechJeb2 <https://github.com/MuMech/MechJeb2>`_ via reflection.

Huge thanks to the authors of `KRPC <https://krpc.github.io/krpc>`_ and `MechJeb2 <https://github.com/MuMech/MechJeb2>`_ for their amazing mods, and to the author of the original `krpcmj <https://github.com/artwhaley/krpcmj/>`_ addon.
